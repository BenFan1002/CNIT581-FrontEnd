Overview

This is a web-based application designed to support the learnersourcing workflow used in the PL/SQL programming class. As students, you will be referred as an "Expert" in your familiar contexts. Your task will be to create your own worked-out example by applying specific course topics into a real-world scenario that you are familiar with. In addition, you will also need to work on your classmate's generated worked-out examples and conduct peer review.
